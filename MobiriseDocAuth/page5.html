Met dit experiment onderzocht KBC de mogelijkheid om de blockchain-technologie in te zetten in de relatie tussen particuliere koper en verkoper en diverse andere partijen die in dit proces betrokken zijn.
Het experiment met blockchain-technologie bleek de basis te kunnen leggen voor een ecosysteem om onze klant bij de aankoop van een auto volledig te ‘ontzorgen’ ten opzichte van zowel de garagist als bank … Op deze domeinen verbetert de KBC My car toepassing de klantervaring door hem niet telkens opnieuw dezelfde gegevens te vragen. Het is de bedoeling om dit in de toekomst nog uit te breiden naar andere partijen die betrokken zijn in het aankoopproces van een wagen, waarbij de klant niet telkens zelf informatie moet verstrekken.

Process flow

Step 0. Optional: KBC creates a car loan.

On request of the customer, the KBC officer creates a car loan. The loan amount, the reference number, number of months and the interest rate are stored on the blockchain. 

Step 1. Car dealer enters a car order.

The order is stored on the blockchain, together with the customer name and the order document hash. The status for this order is now "order initiated".

Step 2. Customer confirms car order.

The customer signs in the application, sees the car-order and confirms this order. This gives the status for this order "order confirmed".

Step 3. Choose your financial options.

The customer can consult his financial options:
o Existing loan
o Last loan simulation
o Link to KBC loan simulation page.

Step 4. Confirmation of delivery

Both the car dealer and the customer confirm the delivery of a car. Update status on the blockchain "finished".

Step 5. Initiation of payment

The customer can initiate the payment when both the car dealer and the customer confirmed the delivery of the car. A payment file can be created.

The smart contract.

What is a smart contract?

Built upon the blockchain, a smart contract is pre-written logic in the form of computer code that is stored, executed and run by the network of computers running the blockchain.
By offering ultimate transparancy as well as eliminating the middleman, smart contracts could have a profound impact on different industries.

On the most basic level, they can:

  1. Store information 
  2. Perform calculations 
  3. Sent transactions to other accounts 

But most importantly, smart contracts are autonomous.

What does it look like?

While there is a blockchain in the background, the user does not need to know the technology. The web-application can be designed just like any other web-application.
